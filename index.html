Welcome to FallSafe!

FallSafe is a physics simulation that tests the safety of gear in a climbing fall.

To get started, try playing around with the slider bars on the left!

Here's a rundown of what the bars do:

  • Climber Height: conrols the height of the climber above the belayer.

  • Protection Height: determines the height of the highest piece of protection (e.g. bolt, piton, cam, nut) above the belayer.

  • Climber Mass: adjusts the mass of the climber.

  • Strength Rating: Changes the amount of force the protection can take before pulling out. Some real-world strength ratings:

    • New Bolt: 25-35 kN
    • Large Cam: 14 kN
    • Small Nut: 5 kN

Once you adjust a slider, just sit back and watch that little guy take a big whip!
